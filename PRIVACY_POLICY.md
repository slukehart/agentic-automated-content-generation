# Privacy Policy for Automatic Content Generation

**Last Updated:** December 16, 2025

## Introduction

Automatic Content Generation ("we," "our," or "us") operates an AI-powered news content automation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application to create and distribute AI-generated news content on TikTok and other social media platforms.

By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- TikTok account credentials (via OAuth - we never see your password)
- YouTube account credentials (via OAuth - we never see your password)
- Email address (if provided for notifications)

**Content Preferences:**
- News topics of interest
- Posting schedule preferences
- Platform selection (TikTok, YouTube, etc.)

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data:**
- Video generation timestamps
- Upload success/failure logs
- API usage statistics
- Error logs for troubleshooting

**Technical Information:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device information

### 3. Third-Party Data

**News Sources:**
- We retrieve news articles from public news APIs (NewsAPI, etc.)
- Article URLs, titles, and content are processed for summarization
- Original source attribution is maintained

**AI Services:**
- Video content processed by HeyGen (AI avatar generation)
- Text processed by Grok AI (summarization)
- Background images generated by X.AI (Grok)

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

### Primary Functions
1. **Content Creation:** Generate AI-powered news video summaries
2. **Platform Integration:** Upload videos to your TikTok, YouTube, and other social media accounts
3. **Authentication:** Maintain secure OAuth connections to social media platforms
4. **Quality Assurance:** Monitor content generation success and troubleshoot errors

### Secondary Functions
1. **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve our service
2. **Communication:** Send important updates about service changes
3. **Compliance:** Ensure adherence to platform policies and legal requirements

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- OAuth tokens: Stored locally on your device in `~/.credentials/` directory
- Content metadata: Stored locally in `content_manifest.json`
- API keys: Stored in your local `.env` file (never transmitted)

### Security Measures
- **Encryption:** OAuth tokens use industry-standard encryption
- **Access Control:** Only you have access to your local credential files
- **No Cloud Storage:** We do not store your credentials on any server
- **Secure Transmission:** All API calls use HTTPS/TLS encryption

### Data Retention
- **OAuth Tokens:** Retained until you revoke access or delete the application
- **Content Metadata:** Retained locally until you delete the files
- **Logs:** Temporary logs automatically purged after 30 days
- **No Server Data:** We do not maintain centralized databases of user data

## Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

### Social Media Platforms
**TikTok:**
- Privacy Policy: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
- We use TikTok's Content Posting API to upload videos to your account
- You control what gets posted through TikTok's inbox review process

**YouTube:**
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We use YouTube Data API v3 to upload videos to your channel
- You maintain full control over uploaded content

### AI Services
**HeyGen (AI Avatar Videos):**
- Privacy Policy: https://www.heygen.com/privacy
- Processes your text scripts to generate video content
- No personal information is sent to HeyGen

**Grok AI (Text Summarization):**
- Privacy Policy: https://x.ai/legal/privacy-policy
- Processes news articles for summarization
- No personal information is sent to Grok

**NewsAPI (News Aggregation):**
- Privacy Policy: https://newsapi.org/privacy
- Public news articles only
- No personal data shared

### Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information is only shared with third-party services as necessary to provide our core functionality, and only as described above.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **Access:** Review all data stored locally on your device
- **Modify:** Change your preferences and settings at any time
- **Delete:** Remove all local data by deleting the application files
- **Export:** Access your content metadata in JSON format

### OAuth Token Management
You can revoke our access to your social media accounts at any time:
- **TikTok:** Visit https://www.tiktok.com/setting/privacy-and-security/personalization
- **YouTube:** Visit https://myaccount.google.com/permissions

Revoking access will immediately prevent our application from posting to your accounts.

### Opt-Out Options
- Stop using the application at any time
- Delete OAuth tokens: `rm ~/.credentials/*.json`
- Delete all generated content and metadata
- Revoke API access through platform settings

## Data Security

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security practices:
- Local-only credential storage (no cloud transmission)
- Encrypted OAuth token storage
- Secure HTTPS connections for all API calls
- No centralized user database
- Open-source codebase for transparency

### Data Breaches
In the unlikely event of a security incident:
- We will notify affected users via email (if provided)
- We will provide guidance on securing your accounts
- We will investigate and patch any vulnerabilities

### Your Responsibility
To maintain security:
- Keep your device secure with password/biometric protection
- Do not share your `.env` file or OAuth tokens
- Regularly review authorized applications on TikTok and YouTube
- Keep the application updated to the latest version
